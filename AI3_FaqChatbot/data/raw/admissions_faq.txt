KALNET SCHOOL — ADMISSIONS FAQ

Q1: What classes does the school offer?
A1: We offer Classes 1 to 12 under the CBSE curriculum.

Q2: What is the last date to apply for admissions?
A2: Applications close on 31 March each year.

Q3: What documents are needed for admission?
A3: Birth certificate, previous year report card, passport-size photos (4), and Aadhaar card copy.

Q4: Is there an entrance test?
A4: Yes, Classes 6 to 10 require a written test in Maths and English.

Q5: What is the age limit for Class 1 admission?
A5: The child must be at least 6 years old as of 31 March of the academic year.

Q6: Are seats available for mid-year admission?
A6: Mid-year admissions are available only if seats are vacant and require principal approval.

Q7: What is the student-teacher ratio?
A7: The ratio is 30:1 for all classes.

Q8: Does the school provide transport?
A8: Yes, transport is available for routes within 15 km of the school.

Q9: What is the medium of instruction?
A9: English is the primary medium. Hindi is compulsory from Classes 1 to 8.

Q10: Is there a hostel facility?
A10: The school does not currently have a hostel facility.

Q11: What sports facilities are available?
A11: Cricket ground, basketball court, badminton court, and a swimming pool for Classes 6 and above.

Q12: What is the school uniform?
A12: White shirt, navy blue trousers or skirt, and black shoes on weekdays. House colour T-shirts on Saturdays.

Q13: Does the school have CCTV?
A13: Yes, all corridors, classrooms, and the main gate are under 24-hour CCTV surveillance.

Q14: How are parents informed about their child's progress?
A14: PTMs are held quarterly. A parent portal is available for real-time attendance and marks.

Q15: Is RTE admission available?
A15: Yes, 25% seats in Classes 1 to 8 are reserved under RTE. Apply through the state government portal.

Q16: When are entrance test results announced?
A16: Results are announced within 7 working days of the test.

Q17: Is there a waiting list system?
A17: Yes, applicants who meet criteria but do not get seats are placed on a waiting list.

Q18: Are laptops or tablets required?
A18: Not mandatory for Classes 1 to 5. Recommended from Class 6 onwards.

Q19: How do I schedule a school visit?
A19: Call the school office between 9 AM and 1 PM Monday to Friday.

Q20: What is the last date to pay fees?
A20: Term 1 fees are due by 15 June. Term 2 by 15 October. Term 3 by 15 January.

KALNET SCHOOL — RULES AND CODE OF CONDUCT

ATTENDANCE:
Minimum 75% attendance is required to appear in final exams.
Leave applications must be submitted in writing before the absence.
Medical leave requires a doctor certificate for absences of 3 or more days.

MOBILE PHONES:
Mobile phones are strictly prohibited for Classes 1 to 8.
Classes 9 to 12 may carry phones but must keep them switched off during class hours.
Violation results in phone confiscation for the term.

EXAMINATIONS:
Cheating during exams leads to immediate cancellation of that paper.
Repeat offences result in suspension.

LIBRARY:
Books may be borrowed for 14 days.
Late return fine: Rs 5 per day.
Damage to library books will be charged at full replacement cost.

CANTEEN:
The canteen is open during lunch break 12:30 PM to 1:15 PM only.
Students must not leave campus to purchase food.

BULLYING:
Zero tolerance for bullying. Any incident must be reported to the class teacher.
First offence: written warning.
Second offence: parents called.
Third offence: suspension.

UNIFORM:
Full uniform is compulsory Monday to Friday.
No jewellery except small studs for girls.
Hair must be neatly tied for girls. Boys must have short hair.

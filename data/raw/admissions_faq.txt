Admissions FAQ

What is the last date to submit the admission form?
The last date to submit the admission form is 30 May 2026.

What documents are required for admission?
Students must submit the previous class report card, birth certificate, transfer certificate if applicable, and two passport-size photographs.

What is the age requirement for Class 1?
Students applying for Class 1 should be at least 6 years old by 31 March 2026.

When are admission interviews conducted?
Admission interviews are scheduled between 10 May 2026 and 18 May 2026.

How can parents contact the admissions office?
Parents can contact the admissions office by visiting the school office during working hours or by emailing admissions@kalnetschool.edu.

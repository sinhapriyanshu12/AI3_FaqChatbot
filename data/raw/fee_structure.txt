KALNET SCHOOL — FEE STRUCTURE 2025-26

Classes 1 to 5:
Tuition Fee: Rs 12,000 per term (3 terms per year)
Transport Fee: Rs 2,500 per month (optional)
Activity Fee: Rs 1,500 per term

Classes 6 to 12:
Tuition Fee: Rs 18,000 per term
Transport Fee: Rs 3,500 per month (optional)
Activity Fee: Rs 2,000 per term

One-time Admission Fee: Rs 5,000 (non-refundable)
Security Deposit: Rs 10,000 (refundable on leaving)

Fee payment deadlines:
Term 1: 15 June
Term 2: 15 October
Term 3: 15 January

Late payment penalty: Rs 100 per day after due date.
Payment modes: Online transfer, cheque payable to KALNET School, or cash at counter.
Fee revision: Fees may be revised annually by up to 10% as per CBSE guidelines.
